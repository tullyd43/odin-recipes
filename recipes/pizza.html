Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a fantastic Detroit Style pizza that you are sure to love. Thick, crispy crust topped with a brightly seasoned fresh tomato sauce. Then smothered in lots of cheese and topped with your favorite pizza toppings.

Ingredients

  • 200g high protein flour
  • 150g water
  • 4g salt
  • 28oz tomato sauce
  • 6oz tomato paste
  • 10oz mozzarella cheese
  • Your shoice of toppings
  • spices to taste

Steps

  1. Add flour, water, and salt to large mixing bowl or stand mixer and knead for approximately 5 minutes
  2. Cover and let dough rise for 30 minutes or until doubles in size
  3. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and your choice of spices to small pot. Mix well and heat until simmering then remove from heat.
  4. Press out dough into a well oiled baking pan. Making sure to stretch dough into corners
  5. Add, enough sauce to cover dough. Then top with cheese. Making sure to cover the entire pizza and line the side up against the pan
  6. Finish by adding your choice of toppings
  7. Bake for approximately 20 minutes at 550*
